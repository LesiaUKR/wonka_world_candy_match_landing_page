How to Play

  • Match three or more similar candies to clear them from the board

  • Complete specific objectives given at the start of each level

  • Earn stars by finishing levels with high scores to unlock decorations

  • Rebuild and decorate various rooms in the factory

  • Progress through the storyline to unlock new characters and areas

  • Participate in regular events and challenges for exclusive rewards.
