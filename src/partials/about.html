About the game

  • Wonka's World of Candy invites players into the fantastical candy factory of Willy Wonka to solve match-3 puzzles that unlock new rooms, machines, and surprises. With over a thousand levels to play through, players can rebuild the chocolate factory to its former glory and beyond.

  • Each level offers unique candy and confectionery challenges with exciting power-ups and combos. As players progress, they can decorate the factory to their taste while following a delightful storyline featuring classic characters like Charlie, Grandpa Joe, and others from the beloved story. Enhanced with vibrant graphics and enchanting music, the game promises a magical journey through a world of endless sweets and imagination.
