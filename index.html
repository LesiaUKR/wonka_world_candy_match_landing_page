About the game

  • Wonka's World of Candy invites players into the fantastical candy factory of Willy Wonka to solve match-3 puzzles that unlock new rooms, machines, and surprises. With over a thousand levels to play through, players can rebuild the chocolate factory to its former glory and beyond.

  • Each level offers unique candy and confectionery challenges with exciting power-ups and combos. As players progress, they can decorate the factory to their taste while following a delightful storyline featuring classic characters like Charlie, Grandpa Joe, and others from the beloved story. How to Play

  • Match three or more similar candies to clear them from the board

  • Complete specific objectives given at the start of each level

  • Earn stars by finishing levels with high scores to unlock decorations

  • Rebuild and decorate various rooms in the factory

  • Participate in regular events and challenges for exclusive rewards.
